About TECHNO PROJECT HUB

TECHNO PROJECT HUB is an educational platform dedicated to helping students, hobbyists, and electronics enthusiasts learn through practical projects.


We provide easy-to-follow tutorials on Arduino, ESP32, IoT, Embedded Systems, Robotics, Tinkercad, Wokwi simulation, and PCB design. Our goal is to make electronics learning simple, practical, and enjoyable.


Our Mission

To empower students with hands-on electronics knowledge through high-quality tutorials, real-world projects, and detailed documentation.
